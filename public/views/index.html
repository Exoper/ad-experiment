ABOUT THE STUDY

Aim

The study aims to identify a relationship between the user response,ad-shareability and the link with the ad-content.

Declaration

Your information will not be shared publicly  and will only be used for the purpose of  the experiment.

Information Collected

In the experiment we collect the facial response from the video recording of user , along with the eye tracking data.

Duration

The entire experiment takes rougly 20-25 mins to complete and is required to be completed in one session.

Instructions

Please read all the instructions carefully . Please ensure that you provide your honest and candid response while taking the experiment.

1. Make sure have you have given the pre-survey before taking the experiment.  
2. After watching every video-advertisemnet, please click on the Continue button and fill in the short questionnaire.
3. Once the experiment is over, please don't close the tab immediately. Wait for the video to get uploaded on the server. It roughly takes 5-7 mins; close the tab once a blue Thank You screen appears. You are free to switch tabs during this period.
4. Ensure that you read all the instructions properly that are displayed during the experiment.
5. Please take the experiment in a room with sufficient lighting.

Hope you enjoy the experiment!
